Be sober-minded; be watchful. Your adversary the devil prowls around like a roaring lion, seeking someone to devour. But test everything; hold fast what is good. Therefore an overseer must be above reproach, the husband of one wife, sober-minded, self-controlled, respectable, hospitable, able to teach.
